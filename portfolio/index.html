Juan Segura

Juan Segura is a San Francisco based visual and user experience designer.

Juan combines his design skills with his background in corporate visual merchandising, and art studio, giving him a unique perspective on design. Prior to launching his business, Juan worked as one of the creative directors of Gap inc, and spent his free time volunteering in the LGBTQ community to educate queer youth.
